## App Description
This app will help users better understand their academic preformance by combining GPA calculations with planning and analysis tools. Users will be able to explore different grade outcomes, estimate what grades they need to reach a target GPA, and identify which classes are helping or hurting their GPA the most. The app is designed to make GPA tracking more interactive and useful than a basic calculator.



## Intended Users
This app is intended for students (high school and college) who want to uderstand how individual classes affect their grades, waht classes tehy need to improve on in order to reach a target GPA, and plan for overall improved academic preformance.

## Instructions 
- On GPA_Planner, input user classes(quantity and class name), grade, credit hours, and planned credits in the future to calculate current GPA and AVG GPA needed to maintain "XXX" GPA. 
- On Course_Breakdown, Follow above instructions and add the type of class you are taking. This will then appear at the bottom of the page with statistics on your classes and what bunch of classes your succeed in the most and the least. 
- The GPA_Math_Breakdown page allows the user to understand how their GPA is calculated and what amount of points equal each letter grade. 
- In Finals_Calculator, input your course name, final exam weight, current grade, and you target grade in that class. Continue to adjust the target grade to see what grade you will need to end at your target.
